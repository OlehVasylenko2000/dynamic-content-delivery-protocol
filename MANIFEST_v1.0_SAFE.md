SECTION 3. CORE INVENTIVE CONCEPT (HIGH-LEVEL)
------------------------------------------------------------

The invention concerns a computer-implemented system and method
for enabling post-distribution updating of digital content
without replacement of the original distributed container.

Key conceptual aspects include:
- separation of a static content container from a versioned
  external content repository;
- cryptographic binding between container and repository;
- authenticated retrieval and application of updates;
- preservation of integrity, access control, and user data.

All technical realization details are defined exclusively
in the patent specification and claims.
